## Description
Stop chasing approvals through endless email threads. ApproveSnap gives freelancers and small agencies a structured, mobile-first approval workflow that gets client sign-offs done faster.

Whether you are a designer sending mockups, a consultant delivering milestones, or a project manager tracking deliverables, ApproveSnap replaces the chaos of scattered feedback with one clear approval process.

KEY FEATURES

Magic Link Sharing
Send encrypted, time-limited approval links to your clients. They review and approve with one tap - no app download or account required. Just click, review, and decide.

Deadline Tracking with Reminders
Set deadlines on every approval item and let automatic reminders do the chasing for you. Notifications at 24 hours, 12 hours, and 2 hours before deadline mean nothing falls through the cracks.

Full Audit Trail
Every approval, rejection, comment, and status change is timestamped and logged. You always have a complete record of who approved what and when - perfect for compliance and accountability.

Approval State Machine
Structured status transitions prevent errors and keep your workflow clean. Items move through Pending, Approved, Rejected, and Changes Requested states with enforced rules.

Project Dashboard
See all your approval projects at a glance. Filter by status, deadline, or client to stay on top of every deliverable.

Comments and Feedback
Add threaded comments on any approval item. Keep all feedback in context so nothing gets lost in email threads or chat messages.

Multiple Approval Types
Track approvals for documents, designs, milestones, links, and text notes. One app for every type of deliverable you need signed off.
