Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a project?

Tap the "+" button on the Home screen or Projects tab. Fill in the project name, client details, and add approval items. Then tap "Create Project".

How do magic links work?

When you send a project for approval, an encrypted magic link is generated. Share this link with your client. They can approve, reject, or request changes without needing to install the app.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions on your iPhone, or open the App Store and tap your profile. Find ApproveSnap and cancel your subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data remains on your device. You can still view projects but cannot create new ones beyond the free tier limit. You can export or delete your data at any time.

How do I restore purchases?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases". This will sync your subscription status with your Apple ID.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Magic links are encrypted with AES-256-GCM. Local data is protected by iOS data protection. iCloud Sync uses end-to-end encryption when enabled.

Subscription Management

To manage your subscription:

  • Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  • Tap your name at the top
  • Tap "Subscriptions"
  • Find ApproveSnap and manage your subscription

Refund Policy

All purchases and subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store. Refund requests should be directed to Apple Support at support.apple.com.
