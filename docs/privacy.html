Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

1. Information We Collect

ApproveSnap collects minimal data necessary to provide our approval workflow service:

  • Project Data: Project names, client names, client emails, approval items, and deadlines you create within the app.
  • Audit Data: Timestamps and actions taken on approval items for audit trail purposes.
  • Subscription Data: Purchase information processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not directly collect payment information.
  • Feedback Data: When you contact support, we collect your name, email, and message content.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the approval workflow service
  • To generate and validate magic links for client approvals
  • To send deadline reminders via push notifications
  • To maintain audit trails for compliance purposes
  • To respond to support inquiries

3. Data Storage

All project data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. If iCloud Sync is enabled, data is encrypted and stored in your iCloud account, which is protected by Apple's security measures. We do not have access to your iCloud-stored data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Apple processes subscription payments through the App Store
  • Magic links shared with your clients contain encrypted approval tokens

5. Data Retention

Free tier users: Audit logs are retained for 7 days. Pro users: Full audit trail is retained indefinitely within the app. You can delete your data at any time by deleting projects within the app.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your data within the app at any time
  • Delete your data by deleting projects
  • Disable iCloud Sync at any time in Settings
  • Request data deletion by contacting support

7. Children's Privacy

ApproveSnap is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Security

Magic links are encrypted using AES-256-GCM. All data transmitted to Apple services uses TLS encryption. Local data is protected by iOS data protection.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date.
